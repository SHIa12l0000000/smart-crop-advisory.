Organic vs Chemical Fertilizers:
  • Organic: Improves soil fertility, slow nutrient release, eco-friendly, minimal chemical residues, good for long-term soil health.
  • Chemical: Quick nutrient supply, boosts short-term yield, may degrade soil structure over time, risk of residue, requires careful usage.
